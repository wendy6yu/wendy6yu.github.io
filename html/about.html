about

Wendy Yu is a junior at Harvard College studying History of Art and Architecture. It's as historical and theoretical as it sounds, so in her spare time she likes to balance out her brain with creative projects - this is recently most often realized through photography, and less often takes place through [occasionally dangerous] furniture building and interior design projects.

Recently, she's most excited about having started the Harvard Design Collective with a group of 5 other amazing women. You can look forward many exciting workshops, panels, challenges, and more related to HDC in the upcoming year.

She's always looking for new challenges and friends, so please - click on the links in the rightmost column - they're lonely!
