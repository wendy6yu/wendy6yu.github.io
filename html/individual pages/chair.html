first impressions

Given 10 10ft 2x4 planks of wood, my initial reaction was to fragment this linearity into something more complementary to the human body.

The solution was therefore to create 2 cradling curves that, when intersected, assumed a third curve that a sitting person could dissolve gently into.

With respect to process, a right profile was first created and then extruded across for volume as far as the 10 10ft planks of wood would allow. Joinery for the most part consisted of Domino mortise and tenon joints.
